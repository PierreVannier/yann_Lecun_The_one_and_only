Working Paper

A Path Towards Autonomous Machine Intelligence

June 2022

How could machines learn as efficiently as humans and animals? This position paper proposes an architecture combining configurable predictive world models, intrinsic motivation, and hierarchical joint embedding architectures trained with self-supervised learning.
